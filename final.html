Our Mission

As a group of young women participating in Girls Who Code (East Palo Alto), we have recognized that the special needs community is often neglected. By creating this simple application, we hope to include anyone with disabilities to better adapt to society. We welcome people of all ethnicities, genders, and backgrounds. It is very important to us that our community is understanding and safe.
