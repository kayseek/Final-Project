Viveka Agrawal

Co Founder

Viveka is an upcoming senior at Washington High School in Fremont, California. In her sophomore year, she had the opportunity to volunteer with the Special Education students at her elementary school, and instantly felt a connection with them. Viveka knew she wanted to do something for the Special Needs community, and this website is a way of allowing people to connect and learn more about what other people go through.

Emily Lam

Co Founder

Emily is an incoming 11th grade student at Washington High School. She is driven to help change the world. After recognizing the inequality at her high school, she knew she had to make everyone at her school feel included. Emily recently started coding at the Girls Who Code where she met a group of inspiring girls looking to uplift each other.
